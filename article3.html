Designing a Digital ID App for the Detroit Tigers: A Case Study

An inside look at how we revolutionized the fan experience through mobile technology for one of MLB's most historic franchises.

Vinamra Tiwari
May 15, 2025 6 min read

The intersection of sports and technology continues to evolve at a remarkable pace. In early 2025, our team was presented with an exciting challenge: design a comprehensive digital ID application for the Detroit Tigers that would transform how fans interact with the historic franchise. This project represented more than just another mobile appâ€”it was an opportunity to reimagine the entire stadium experience.

From streamlining entry at Comerica Park to personalizing fan engagement, the Digital Tigers ID needed to balance sophisticated functionality with an intuitive interface that would appeal to fans of all ages. This article explores our journey through this complex design process and the innovative solutions we implemented.

1. Understanding the Fan Ecosystem

Before writing a single line of code, we immersed ourselves in the Tigers fan experience. We conducted extensive research at Comerica Park, interviewing everyone from season ticket holders to casual visitors, stadium operations staff, and team management to understand the diverse needs of all stakeholders.

What emerged was a complex ecosystem with different pain points for various user groups. Season ticket holders wanted expedited entry and exclusive perks, families needed simplified navigation for multiple tickets, while first-time visitors required accessible information about stadium amenities. The challenge was creating a unified solution that served all these audience segments without compromising on simplicity.

"We didn't want to build just another sports app. We wanted to create something that truly feels like an extension of the Tigers brand and enhances what makes attending a game at Comerica Park special."

2. Core Identity Verification Technology

The foundation of the Digital Tigers ID is its secure identity verification system. Working closely with the team's security department, we implemented a multi-factor authentication system that prioritizes both security and convenience. The resulting solution utilizes biometric verification combined with dynamically updating visual and NFC-based verification tokens.

The verification system now powers:

  • Contactless stadium entry with dedicated fast-track lanes
  • Secure merchandise and concession purchases
  • Age verification for alcohol purchases
  • Premium area and special event access control

3. Integrated Loyalty Experience

Beyond basic identification, the app features a comprehensive loyalty program that rewards fans for their engagement with the team. Rather than creating a separate points system, we integrated the loyalty features directly into the ID functionality, creating a seamless experience that encourages ongoing interaction.

The loyalty element automatically tracks attendance, purchases, and participation in team activities, converting these interactions into tiered status levels that unlock increasingly valuable benefits. This approach has proven particularly effective for increasing mid-week game attendance and encouraging fans to arrive earlier to the ballpark.

Key Loyalty Components:

  1. Attendance-based rewards that accumulate across seasons
  2. Special recognition for milestone games (25th, 50th, 100th, etc.)
  3. Exclusive digital collectibles unlocked through stadium activities
  4. Priority access to special events and limited promotions

4. Personalized Fan Experience Portal

One of the most successful elements of the Digital Tigers ID has been its dynamic personalization capabilities. The app studies user behavior and preferences to create tailored experiences for each fan, while still respecting privacy boundaries and giving users control over their data sharing preferences.

This personalization extends beyond simple recommendations to create truly unique game day experiences based on historical preferences, current promotions, and real-time stadium conditions. Weather alerts, concession wait times, and bathroom line updates are all customized based on the user's current location and past behavior patterns.

The personalization system has dramatically increased in-app engagement, with users spending an average of 27 minutes interacting with the app during each gameâ€”far exceeding industry benchmarks for sports applications.

5. Accessibility and Inclusion Focus

From the beginning, we committed to making the Digital Tigers ID accessible to all fans. Working closely with disability advocates and implementing WCAG 2.2 guidelines, we created an experience that works for everyone, regardless of their abilities or technological comfort level.

The app includes features like high-contrast mode, screen reader optimization, simplified navigation options, and multiple language support including Spanish and Arabic to serve Detroit's diverse population. Importantly, the stadium also maintains alternative verification methods for fans without smartphones, ensuring no one is excluded from the game day experience.

Conclusion

The Detroit Tigers Digital ID app represents a significant step forward in how sports franchises can leverage technology to enhance the fan experience. By focusing on solving real pain points rather than implementing technology for its own sake, we've created a solution that genuinely improves the connection between fans and the team they love.

Early metrics show promising resultsâ€”entry times have decreased by 61%, concession sales are up 24%, and user satisfaction scores lead the league. As we continue refining the experience based on real-world usage and feedback, the Digital Tigers ID will evolve alongside fan needs, ensuring that Comerica Park remains at the forefront of the modern baseball experience while honoring the rich history and traditions that make Tigers baseball special.
